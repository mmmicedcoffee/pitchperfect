About Us

Welcome to Pitch Perfect, a place for a cappella members to practice their pieces outside of group practice time, with an emphasis on social interactions among the group.

Have you ever had difficulty perfecting your individual parts when singing in a musical group? This site allows you to practice your parts with the other voice parts in the background to simulate singing with the whole a cappella group. Members of your group can then listen to your recordings and make comments.

Feel free to contact us with any feedback: pitch-perfect@mit.edu.
